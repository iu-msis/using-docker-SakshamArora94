Hi, My name is Batman. I fight crime and beat criminals to a pulp with my bare hands. I prefer breaking their bones and leaving them in agonizing pain for weeks to come rather than granting them a quick painless death. I also love destroying property using my indestructible car which I call, you guessed it, batmobile (How original am I?)

In the process of "trying" to save my city (from god knows what), I ended up bankrupting my father's company. If only I had attended Kelley school of business, I could have stayed ahead of the curve by implementing disruptive technologies in my company. Instead I burned through all the money on cool toys for myself.

Oh and one last thing. I'm a grown man on the outside, but I'm still a kid on the inside, which is why I have my personal nanny (I call him a butler) who's been taking care of me since I was born. He's as old as Queen Elizabeth II but I still make him cook me food and do my clothes
